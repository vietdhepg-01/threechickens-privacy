Data Deletion

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Current Lite release

The current Lite release does not create a player account. Game progress and settings may be stored locally on your Android device.

Delete local game data

  1. Open Android Settings.
  2. Open Apps and select Idle PT Champ.
  3. Open Storage.
  4. Select Clear storage or Clear data.

Uninstalling the Game will also normally remove its local data.

Request deletion of support information

If you previously contacted us and want us to delete information contained in that support conversation, email privacy@ultimateindex.asia with the subject Idle PT Champ Data Deletion Request.

Include enough information for us to locate the relevant correspondence. Do not send passwords, government identification, payment card data, or other unnecessary sensitive information.

Processing

We will review valid requests and respond within a reasonable period, subject to identity verification and any information we must retain for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or recordkeeping purposes.
