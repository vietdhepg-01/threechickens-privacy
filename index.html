Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Three Chickens Studio (“we,” “us,” or “our”) publishes Idle PT Champ (the “Game”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game.

1. Scope of this policy

This policy applies to the current Lite release of Idle PT Champ distributed through Google Play. It does not apply to websites, applications, or services operated by third parties.

2. Information handled by the current Lite release

The current Lite release does not require an account and is designed to work without advertising or third-party analytics. Game progress and settings may be stored locally on your device.

We do not intentionally collect names, postal addresses, precise location, contacts, photos, payment information, or other sensitive personal information through the current Lite release.

3. Local game data

The Game may save gameplay progress, preferences, and configuration data on your device. This information remains on your device unless a future version introduces an online save feature. You can normally remove local data by clearing the Game’s storage or uninstalling the Game.

4. Google Play and device services

When you download or use the Game through Google Play, Google and your device platform may independently process technical and transactional information under their own privacy policies. This may include device identifiers, download information, security information, diagnostics, purchase records, or other platform data. We do not control Google’s independent processing.

5. How information is used

Information handled by the Game is used only to:

  • operate the Game and preserve local progress;
  • maintain security and prevent abuse;
  • diagnose technical problems when you contact support; and
  • comply with legal obligations.

6. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. The current Lite release does not share personal information with advertising networks or data brokers.

Information may be disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with law, enforce our rights, protect users, investigate abuse, or respond to a valid request from a competent authority.

7. Data retention and deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you clear the Game’s storage or uninstall the Game. Support correspondence may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary to resolve the request, maintain records, prevent abuse, or meet legal obligations.

The current Lite release does not create a player account. Instructions for deleting local data and submitting a privacy request are available on our Data Deletion page.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures appropriate to the information we handle. No storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children’s privacy

The Game is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the current Lite release. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

10. International users

The Game may be available in multiple countries. Any support information you voluntarily provide may be processed in Vietnam or in another country where our service providers operate, subject to applicable safeguards.

11. Future features and policy updates

Future versions may add optional services such as cloud saves, sign-in, analytics, advertising, purchases, or multiplayer features. Before enabling a feature that changes our data practices, we will update this policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration as required.

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above shows when the latest revision took effect.
